Frogs
Glass Frog Tree Frog

Frogs are captivting and fun animals. Everyone know what a frog looks like, but they do not always know how interesting they are. Frogs are amphibians that are known for their jumping abilities, croaking sounds, bulging eyes and slimy skin. They live all over the world and are among the most diverse animals in the world Frogs encompass over 6,000 species in the world. We will focus on two main species: the Glass Frog and the Tree Frog.
